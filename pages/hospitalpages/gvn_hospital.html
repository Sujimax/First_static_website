Government Hospital - Uthukottai

Hospital Details

  1. Established: 1965
  2. Type: Government General Hospital
  3. Ownership: Tamil Nadu State Government
  4. Location: Near Bus Stand, Uthukottai
  5. Hospital Timings: 24x7 Emergency Services
  6. Outpatient (OP) Timings: 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM

About the Hospital

The Government Hospital in Uthukottai provides essential medical services to the local and surrounding rural population. With qualified doctors and well-maintained facilities, the hospital plays a vital role in public health, maternal care, and emergency treatment.

Departments Available

  1. General Medicine
  2. Pediatrics
  3. Gynecology & Obstetrics
  4. Orthopedics
  5. Dentistry
  6. Emergency & Trauma Care
  7. Vaccination & Immunization

Facilities

  1. 24x7 Emergency Ward
  2. Outpatient (OP) Block
  3. Ambulance Service
  4. Laboratory & Diagnostic Tests
  5. Pharmacy (Government Free Medicines)
  6. Separate Wards for Male, Female & Children
  7. Maternity Ward & Delivery Room

Special Services

  1. Free Vaccination for Infants and Pregnant Women
  2. Pulse Polio Campaigns
  3. Health Awareness Camps
  4. Monthly Mobile Health Unit to nearby villages
