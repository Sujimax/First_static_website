About the School

Vivekananda Matriculation Higher Secondary School, located in Uthukottai, was founded in 1985 with the mission of delivering quality education to students in a rural setting. It has grown from a modest beginning to a full-fledged higher secondary institution.

The school is known for academic excellence, discipline, and extracurricular achievements. It operates under the motto of Swami Vivekananda: "Arise, awake, and stop not till the goal is reached."
