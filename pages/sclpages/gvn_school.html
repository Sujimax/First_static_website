Government Girls Higher Secondary School - Uthukottai

School Details

  1. Established: 1975
  2. Type: Government, Girls Only
  3. Management: Tamil Nadu State Government
  4. Medium of Instruction: Tamil and English
  5. Classes Offered: 6th to 12th standard
  6. School Timings: 9:00 AM to 4:30 PM

About the School

The Government Girls Higher Secondary School in Uthukottai is dedicated to empowering young women through education. Since its establishment in 1975, the school has been a pillar of learning for girls from nearby villages, offering strong academic programs and personal development opportunities.

Facilities

  1. Spacious Classrooms
  2. Well-equipped Science Laboratories
  3. Computer Lab with Internet Access
  4. Girls Hostel Facility (for select students)
  5. Sanitary Facilities & Clean Drinking Water
  6. Library with Tamil & English books
  7. Playground and Assembly Hall

Achievements

  1. Consistently high performance in 10th and 12th Board Exams
  2. Participation in State-level cultural and sports events
  3. Alumni pursuing careers in teaching, nursing, and government services

Location

Address: Government Girls Higher Secondary School, Uthukottai, Tiruvallur District, Tamil Nadu
