About the School

Don Bosco Matriculation Higher Secondary School, located in Uthukottai, was founded in 1958 by the Salesians of Don Bosco. The school began with only 140 students under the leadership of Fr. Mallon, the first principal. Over the decades, it has grown significantly to serve over 5000 students.

The school is known for providing a disciplined, value-based education along with academic excellence. It focuses on the all-round development of students through academics, sports, leadership programs, and moral guidance.
