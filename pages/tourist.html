Karaneeshvarar Temple is a serene and ancient Shiva temple located in Uthukottai. Known for its peaceful ambiance and traditional architecture, it attracts local devotees and visitors looking for spiritual calm.

Main Deity : Lord Shiva (Karaneeshvarar)

Specialty : Peaceful environment and daily poojas

Tourist Place : Surutapalli Shivan Temple

Surutapalli Shivan Temple is a rare and ancient temple near Uthukottai where Lord Shiva is depicted in a reclining posture. This unique feature attracts pilgrims and tourists interested in spirituality and temple architecture.

Specialty : Reclining Lord Shiva, historic temple architecture

Tourist Place : Arani River

The Arani River flows near Uthukottai and is an important natural resource for the region. It provides water for agriculture and supports local ecosystems. The river banks are peaceful spots for visitors to enjoy nature and relax.

Importance : Agriculture, natural beauty, local habitat

Location : Arani River near Uthukottai
