Nagalamman Amman Temple - Uthukottai

Temple Details

  1. Deity: Goddess Nagalamman
  2. Temple Type: Village Amman Temple
  3. Location: Uthukottai Town
  4. Timings: 6:00 AM to 12:00 PM & 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM
  5. Festivals: Aadi Festival, Panguni Uthiram
  6. Contact: +91 98430 12345

About the Temple

Nagalamman Temple is a powerful village shrine in Uthukottai. Devotees believe that the goddess protects the town and blesses families with health and prosperity. The temple is especially vibrant during Aadi festivals.

Rituals & Events

  1. Special Friday Pujas
  2. Amman Abhishekam
  3. Navarathri Celebrations
  4. Free Annadhanam during festivals

Facilities

  1. Rest Areas for Devotees
  2. Pooja Items Stall Nearby
  3. Free Drinking Water
  4. Women’s Waiting Hall
  5. Public Toilets

Location

Address: Nagalamman Amman Temple, Uthukottai, Tiruvallur District, Tamil Nadu
