Perumal Temple - Uthukottai

Temple Details

  1. Deity: Lord Vishnu (Perumal)
  2. Temple Type: Ancient Vaishnavite Temple
  3. Location: Uthukottai, near bus stand
  4. Timings: 6:30 AM to 12:00 PM & 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM
  5. Festivals: Vaikunta Ekadasi, Purattasi Saturdays
  6. Contact: +91 98765 54321

About the Temple

Perumal Temple in Uthukottai is a peaceful shrine dedicated to Lord Vishnu. The temple follows Vaishnavite traditions and attracts devotees during Purattasi month and Vaikunta Ekadasi.

Rituals & Events

  1. Vishnu Sahasranamam Recitation
  2. Special Alankaram during Saturdays
  3. Deepa Aradhanai and Archana
  4. Distribution of Theertham and Prasadam

Facilities

  1. Open Mandapam for Gatherings
  2. Drinking Water
  3. Seating Area for Elderly
  4. Footwear Stand
  5. Temple Office

Location

Address: Perumal Temple, Uthukottai, Tiruvallur District, Tamil Nadu
