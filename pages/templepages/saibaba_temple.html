Thiru Neelakandeswarar Swamy Temple - Uthukottai

Temple Details

  1. Deity: Lord Shiva (Thiru Neelakandeswarar)
  2. Consort: Goddess Neelakandeswari Amman
  3. Location: Uthukottai Town, near Bazaar Street
  4. Temple Type: Ancient Shiva Temple
  5. Timings: 6:00 AM – 12:00 PM & 4:00 PM – 8:30 PM
  6. Major Festivals: Maha Shivaratri, Pradosham, Karthigai Deepam
  7. Contact: +91 98765 67890

About the Temple

Thiru Neelakandeswarar Swamy Temple is one of the most ancient and significant temples in Uthukottai, dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple has spiritual importance and is a center of devotion for the local people.

The name "Neelakandeswarar" refers to Lord Shiva who drank the poison (halahala) during Samudra Manthan, turning his throat blue. The temple also houses Goddess Neelakandeswari as His consort.

Rituals & Events

  1. Daily Pujas and Rudrabhishekam
  2. Pradosham twice a month
  3. Maha Shivaratri with whole-night bhajans
  4. Karthigai Deepam with grand celebrations
  5. Navarathri and temple processions

Facilities

  1. Spacious Prakaram (Temple Courtyard)
  2. Resting Area for Devotees
  3. Drinking Water
  4. Shoe Stand
  5. Free Annadhanam on special days

Location

Address: Thiru Neelakandeswarar Swamy Temple, Uthukottai, Tiruvallur District, Tamil Nadu
