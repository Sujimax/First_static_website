Arani River - Uthukottai

River Overview

The Arani River is a seasonal river that flows through Uthukottai, providing water for agriculture and acting as a natural attraction. It's part of the Araniyar River Basin and connects several villages in the region.

Importance

  1. Used for agricultural irrigation
  2. Supports local ecosystem and groundwater levels
  3. Seasonal floods help in soil fertility

Best Time to Visit

Monsoon and post-monsoon months (July to December) when the river has water flow.

Nearby Attractions

  1. River-side walks and local village life
  2. Photography and bird watching during winters
  3. Close to several temples along its banks

Location

Address: Arani River, Uthukottai, Tiruvallur District, Tamil Nadu
